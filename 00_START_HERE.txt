================================================================================
                          QUICK WALKTHROUGH
================================================================================

When you open TBOrder_Kiosk.html:

SCREEN 1: Welcome
  - Shows "The Bap" logo
  - Click "Eat In" or "Take Away"

SCREEN 2: Menu
  - Left sidebar: 7 categories
  - Main area: Menu items (21 total)
  - Click any item to see details

SCREEN 3: Item Details
  - See nutrition facts
  - Choose sauce (8 options)
  - Choose toppings (if available)
  - Adjust quantity
  - Click "Add to Cart"

SCREEN 4: Cart
  - Review all items
  - Modify quantities
  - Remove items if needed
  - Click "Place Order"

SCREEN 5: Payment
  - Choose payment method
  - Card or Cash
  - Click "Confirm Payment"

SCREEN 6: Confirmation
  - Order number (TB-001, TB-002, etc.)
  - Estimated wait time
  - Auto-returns to welcome screen in 10 seconds

                          COMMON QUESTIONS

Q: Do I need to install anything?
A: No! Just open the HTML file in a browser.

Q: Do I need internet?
A: No. It works completely offline.

Q: Can I change the menu?
A: Yes, easily. See IMPLEMENTATION_GUIDE.md

Q: Can I change the colors?
A: Yes, it's just CSS variables.

Q: Does it work on tablets?
A: Yes! Touch-optimized and responsive.

Q: Where are orders saved?

A: Open browser DevTools, export from IndexedDB

A: Yes, everything is local
